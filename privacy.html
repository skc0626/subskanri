Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

“Subscriptions” (the “App”) is built to respect your privacy. This policy explains how the App handles your information.

1. Information we handle

The App stores the subscription details you enter (such as service name, price, and billing date). This information is kept on your device and is never sent to the developer or any third-party server.

2. iCloud sync

If you enable iCloud Sync, your data is synced to other devices signed in to your own Apple ID through Apple’s iCloud. This data is stored in your private iCloud; the developer cannot access it. You can turn sync off at any time in Settings.

3. No personal data, no tracking

The App does not collect your name, email, or location. It does not use advertising identifiers, does not track you, and includes no third-party analytics.

4. Notifications

Reminders for billing dates and free-trial endings are scheduled entirely on your device as local notifications. No data is sent externally to deliver them.

5. In-app purchase

Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. Payment information is handled by Apple; the developer never receives your card or payment details.

6. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email veltrafoundry@gmail.com.

7. Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. Significant changes will be noted in the App or on the App Store.
