Subscriptions — Bill & Cost Tracker

A simple, privacy-first app to track your subscriptions and fixed costs, so you never forget to cancel and never overpay. All data is entered manually — no bank or account linking required.

Key features

  • Reminders before each billing date and before free trials end
  • Automatic monthly and yearly totals, with multi-currency support
  • Spending charts by category and a spending-over-time trend
  • Billing calendar and Home Screen & Lock Screen widgets
  • Pause, archive, tag and note your subscriptions
  • Private iCloud sync across your devices, CSV export & backup

Privacy

Your data stays on your device and syncs only through your own private iCloud. The developer never has access to it. No bank connections, no account required, no tracking.
